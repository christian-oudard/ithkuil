**A GRAMMAR OF NEW ITHKUIL**
**A CONSTRUCTED LANGUAGE**
**INTRODUCTION**
This website presents the grammar of New Ithkuil, a design for a constructed human-usable language.[Ithkuil](https://ithkuil.net/00_intro.html), but has been significantly modified to incorporate various suggestions for improvements from fans of the predecessor language (see **[Appendix C](https://ithkuil.net/newithkuil_appendices.htm#Appendix_C)** for a list of these contributors) as well as new ideas from its author.
